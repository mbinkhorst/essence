ESSENCE® BUN WARMER
    quick reference guide
    ERROR RESET
    In case of an unexpected error message, press and hold the
     
    button for 6 seconds to reset the Bun Warmer.
    BUN WARMER CONTENT
    1. Using foil/wax paper, is only allowed to use when it is heat resistant. Using material with low heat resistance can cause overheat or burning smell situations.
    2. To obtain the best performance, do not overload the Bun Warmer.
    CLEANING
    Make sure that the Bun Warmer cavity is checked and if necessary cleaned after each cooking program.
