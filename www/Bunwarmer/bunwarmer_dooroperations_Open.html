ESSENCE® BUN WARMER
    DOOR OPERATIONS
    HOW TO OPEN THE DOOR
    LH - CLOSE/LOCK POSITION
    RH - CLOSE/LOCK POSITION
    Slide the secondary latch to the upper position.
    LH - SAFE POSITION
    RH - SAFE POSITION
    Turn the primary door latch from the vertical lock position to the
     
    SAFE position. In the
     
    SAFE position, the door is mechanically locked but not ready for operation.
    LH - OPEN POSITION
    RH - OPEN POSITION
    Turn and hold the primary door latch to the
     
    OPEN position and open the door to load or remove the Bun Warmer content.
