    OPEN DOOR DETECTION
    OPEN DOOR DETECTION
    If the door is opened during a program, the heating of the Bun Warmer stops. The light ring of the
     
    button starts to flash. The indicators
     
    of the MODE button go OFF and the timer pauses the selected program.
    Close the door to resume the program. The light ring of the
     
    button stops to flash. The indicator
     
    of the MODE button illuminates and the timer pauses the selected program.
