    How to Pause a program
    During a program, press the
     
    button to pause the program.
    The display shows the pause icon.
    Press the
     
    button again to continue the program. Note: If a program is paused (also when door is opened) longer than 2 minutes, the Oven goes back to the STANDBY ON mode.
