    POD HOLDER RINSE
    OBSERVE the ON/OFF light ring is Green, indicating the unit is in the STANDBY ON mode, and all indicators are steady ON.
    FROTH MODULE AND POD HOLDERS
    1. REMOVE the Froth Module and both Pod Holders.
    2. EMPTY both Pod Holders and RE-INSTALL them in the Espresso/Cappuccino Machine.
    RECEIVING CUPS
    PLACE receiving cups, or the Froth Module Reservoir (without the lid) turned sideways, into the brew cavities to collect water from the Brew Heads.
    BREW HANDLE
    PLACE the Brew Handle in the ‘down’ position. The Espresso/Cappuccino Machine will not allow activation of the CLEAN function if the Brew Handle is not down.
    1. OBSERVE the CLEAN button will be lit.
    2. PRESS and RELEASE the CLEAN button; the Espresso/Cappuccino Machine pumps will turn on.
    RECEIVING CUPS
    OBSERVE the pumps turning on and off for approximately ten seconds. Clean water will flow through the Pod Holders and collect in the receiving cups or Froth Module Reservoir.
    RECEIVING CUPS
    EMPTY the receiving cups or Froth Module Reservoir. Any water collected should be disposed of in the galley sink.

    NOTE: The platen drip tray is not intended for disposal of large volumes of liquid. Do not pour liquids into the drip tray.
    CAUTION!! KEEP ALL FOREIGN OBJECTS OUT OF THE PLATEN AREA AND DRAIN. BLOCKING THIS AREA WITH PAPER OR OTHER DEBRIS COULD CAUSE IT TO CLOG AND OVERFILL.
