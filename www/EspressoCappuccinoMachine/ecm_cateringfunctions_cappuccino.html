    HOW TO BREW CAPPUCCINO

    MILK SHOULD BE REFRIGERATED AT ALL TIMES WHEN NOT IN USE. MILK SHOULD NOT BE STORED IN THE ESPRESSO/CAPPUCCINO MACHINE WHEN THE MILK IS NOT GOING TO BE USED IMMEDIATELY. THE FROTH MODULE MAY BE REFRIGERATED.

    THE BREW CAVITY AND FROTH MODULE SHOULD BE WIPED DOWN AFTER EVERY SERVICE TO REMOVE COFFEE AND MILK RESIDUE. THE FROTH MODULE SHOULD BE RINSED OR CLEANED, AS APPROPRIATE, PER THE INSTRUCTIONS IN SECTION 5.

    OBSERVE the ON/OFF light ring is Green, indicating the unit is in the STANDBY ON mode, and all indicators are steady ON.
    FROTH MODULE
    FILL the Froth Module with fresh milk to the desired level and put the lid on.
    BREW HANDLE AND POD HOLDERS
    RAISE the Brew Handle and REMOVE the left Pod Holder from the Espresso/Cappuccino Machine. Discard any used espresso pod.
    FROTH MODULE
    INSERT the Froth Module into the right side of the Espresso/Cappuccino Machine. The right side brew functions will NOT operate when the Froth Module is in the Espresso/Cappuccino Machine.
    POD HOLDER
    PLACE an ESE espresso pod into one Pod Holder and press down firmly on the pod. INSERT the Pod Holder fully into the left brew cavity of the Espresso/Cappuccino Machine.
    PUSH until Pod Holder “clicks” securely into place.
    RECEIVING CUP
    PLACE a cup into the left receiving cup space. The receiving cup is NOT secured by the Brew Handle.
    BREW HANDLE
    PLACE the Brew Handle in the ‘down’ position. The Espresso/Cappuccino Machine will not operate if the Brew Handle is not down.
    PRESS and RELEASE the Cappuccino button. Frothed milk will be dispensed into the receiving cup, followed by the Espresso brew.
    NOTE: A SELECTED FUNCTION CAN BE CANCELLED BY PRESSING THE FUNCTION BUTTON AGAIN.
