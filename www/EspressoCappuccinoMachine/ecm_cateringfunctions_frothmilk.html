    HOW TO FROTH MILK
    This process can be used to increase frothed milk volume in a beverage, if desired.

    THE BREW CAVITY AND FROTH MODULE SHOULD BE WIPED DOWN AFTER EVERY SERVICE TO REMOVE COFFEE AND MILK RESIDUE. THE FROTH MODULE SHOULD BE RINSED OR CLEANED, AS APPROPRIATE, PER THE INSTRUCTIONS IN SECTION 5.

    FROTH MODULE
    INSERT the Froth Module (with milk) into the Espresso/Cappuccino Machine.
    RECEIVING CUP
    PLACE a cup in the left receiving cup space.
    OBSERVE
    1. For Auto Mode: PRESS and RELEASE the FROTH button for a 20-second froth.
    2. For Manual Mode: PRESS and HOLD the FROTH button for frothing that continues until the FROTH button is released.
    NOTE: Do not hold the FROTH button longer than 60 seconds.
    NOTE: A SELECTED FUNCTION CAN BE CANCELLED BY PRESSING THE FUNCTION BUTTON AGAIN.
    FROTHING MILK WITH THE FROTH WAND
    FROTH MODULE
    REMOVE the Froth Module from the Espresso/Cappuccino Machine. IMPORTANT: If the Froth Module is installed, the Froth Wand will not warm or froth the milk.
    FROTHING PITCHER
    FILL a frothing pitcher with up to 165 mL of milk. Using more than 165 mL of milk will cause the milkâ€™s temperature to be cooler than desired after frothing.

    NOTE: The milk volume will approximately double during the frothing process.
    FROTH WAND
    INSERT Froth Wand into the frothing pitcher of milk.
    OBSERVE the ON/OFF light ring is Green, indicating the unit is in the STANDBY ON mode, and all indicators are steady ON.
    1. For Auto Mode: PRESS and RELEASE the FROTH button for a 60-second froth.
    2. For Manual Mode: PRESS and HOLD the FROTH button for frothing that continues until the FROTH button is released.
    NOTE: Do not hold the FROTH button longer than 60 seconds.
    MILK SHOULD BE REFRIGERATED AT ALL TIMES WHEN NOT IN USE. MILK SHOULD NOT BE STORED IN THE ESPRESSO/CAPPUCCINO MACHINE WHEN THE MILK IS NOT GOING TO BE USED IMMEDIATELY. THE FROTH MODULE MAY BE REFRIGERATED.
    IMPORTANT! THE FROTH WAND MUST BE RINSED OR CLEANED AFTER EACH SERVICE. DO NOT ALLOW MILK TO DRY ON THE INSIDE OR THE OUTSIDE OF THE FROTH WAND. THE FROTH WAND SHOULD BE RINSED OR CLEANED, AS APPROPRIATE, PER THE INSTRUCTIONS IN SECTION 5.
