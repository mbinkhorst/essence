    FROTH MODULE CLEANING
    LIGHTLY CLEAN THE FROTH MODULE WITH A CLEANING SOLUTION PER THE RINSE INSTRUCTIONS IN SECTION 5C. OR, FOR A MORE THOROUGH CLEANING, FOLLOW THE INSTRUCTIONS BELOW.
    FROTH MODULE
    1. REMOVE the Froth Module from the Espresso/Cappuccino Machine (6A).
    2. REMOVE the Froth Module lid (6B).
    3. EMPTY the Froth Module by pouring any unused milk into the galley sink.
    FROTH MODULE - LID COMPO-
    NENTS
    DISASSEMBLE the Aerator and Pickup Tube from the Froth Module lid (6B). The Rubber Grommet can remain attached to the lid.
    FROTH MODULE
    RINSE ALL Froth Module components with clean fresh water.
    FROTH MODULE
    SOAK ALL Froth Module components in cleaning solution* for several minutes.
    FROTH MODULE
    SCRUB ALL Froth Module components with a cleaning brush to remove any remaining debris, as necessary.
    FROTH MODULE
    RINSE ALL Froth Module components thoroughly with clean fresh water.
    FROTH MODULE
    1. RE-ASSEMBLE the Froth Module.
    2. FILL the Froth Module with clean fresh water.
    3. INSERT the Froth Module into the Espresso/Cappuccino Machine.
    RECEIVING CUP
    PLACE a large receiving cup (minimum capacity of 175 mL/6 oz.) under the left side Brew Head.
    PRESS and HOLD the FROTH button, until the receiving cup is full of liquid from the Froth Module.

    NOTE: Do not hold the FROTH button for longer than 60 seconds.
    RECEIVING CUP
    REMOVE the receiving cup and dispose of any liquid collected by pouring it into the galley sink.
    FROTH MODULE
    EMPTY the Froth Module by pouring any remaining liquid into the galley sink.
    FROTH MODULE
    RE-INSTALL the Froth Module into the Espresso/Cappuccino Machine, if desired. The unit is ready for operation.
    * Cleaning solution may be any of the following:
    1. 500mL cold water with 5mL antibacterial dishwashing liquid.
    2. 500mL cold water with 15mL Full Circle® cleaning liquid.
    3. 500mL cold water with 30mL Rinza® cleaning liquid.
    4. Other milk cleaning solutions per cleaning product manufacturer instructions.
