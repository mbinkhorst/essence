    FREEZE PROTECTION PROCEDURE
    OBSERVE the ON/OFF light ring is Green, indicating the unit is in the STANDBY ON mode, and all indicators are steady ON.
    FROTH MODULE AND POD HOLDERS
    1. REMOVE the Froth Module and both Pod Holders.
    2. EMPTY both Pod Holders and RE-INSTALL them in the Espresso/Cappuccino Machine.
    PRESS the ON/OFF button once.
    OBSERVE the ON/OFF light ring is Blue, indicating the unit is in STANDBY OFF mode.
    RECEIVING CUPS
    PLACE receiving cups, or the Froth Module Reservoir (without the lid) turned sideways, into the brew cavities to collect water from the Brew Heads.
    BREW HANDLE
    PLACE the Brew Handle in the ‘down’ position. The Espresso/Cappuccino Machine will not allow activation of the Freeze Protection procedure if the Brew Handle is not down.
    OBSERVE the CLEAN button will be lit. PRESS and HOLD the CLEAN button until the Espresso/Cappuccino Machine pumps turn on (approximately three seconds).
    1. OBSERVE the ON/OFF light ring is Blue and blinking.
    2. OBSERVE the pumps turning on and off, with water exiting the Brew Heads. The water flow will slow, until it nearly or completely stops.
    1. OBSERVE the pumps turn off, and the ON/OFF light ring turns steady Blue.
    2. EMPTY the receiving cups or Froth Module Reservoir. Any water collected should be disposed of in the galley sink.

    NOTE: The platen drip tray is not intended for disposal of large volumes of liquid. Do not pour liquids into the drip tray.
