ESSENCE® Espresso/Cappuccino Machine
    quick reference guide
    All function indicators are NOT LIT

    Brew Handle is not in the ‘down’ position. Lower the Brew Handle.

    Unit is in STANDBY OFF. Press the ON/OFF button to switch to STANDBY ON.

    See also items 8, 9, and 10

    Right side ESPRESSO and RISTRETTO indicators are NOT LIT

    Froth Module is installed. Right side ESPRESSO and RISTRETTO functions are not available, and indicators will not be lit, when Froth Module is installed.

    CAPPUCCINO indicator is NOT LIT

    Froth Module is not installed. Install Froth Module in unit.

    Froth Module is not fully inserted. The locking tabs will be engaged and locked when the module is fully inserted.

    Froth Module sensor magnet may be damaged or missing.

    Repair or replace the Froth Module assembly.

    Function indicator is blinking

    Unit is not up to temperature. Select desired function again when indicator stops blinking.

    Unit will not brew

    Brew Handle is not in the ‘down’ position. Lower the Brew Handle.

    Unit is in STANDBY OFF. Press the ON/OFF button to switch to STANDBY ON.

    See also items 8, 9, and 10

    Espresso or Ristretto brew volume is lower than normal

    Some air could be in the system lines after not being used for extended periods of time in the STANDBY ON mode. More volume can be added by pressing the ESPRESSO or RISTRETTO button to continue the brew until the desired volume has been reached. Press the button again to stop brewing.

    Pod Holders could be clogged with dried coffee or debris. Clean thoroughly, following the instructions in Section 5F, then try brewing again.

    Cappuccino volume (frothed milk) is significantly higher or lower than normal with significantly more foam or no foam

    Froth Module could be clogged with dried coffee, milk, or debris. Rinse the Froth Module, following the instructions in Section 5C, or clean the Froth Module, following the instructions in Section 5G. Afterwards, try brewing again.

    ON/OFF indicator is AMBER

    Unit has a fault. Turn the unit off, then turn it back on. If the fault persists, return the unit for service.

    The NO WATER indicator is LIT

    There is no water supply to unit. Check galley water supply.

    The NETWORK LOSS indicator is LIT

    A loss of network connection between the unit and the aircraft network controller has occurred. Unit may not operate until situation is resolved.

    Unit does not Prime; ON/OFF indicator turns back to BLUE

    There is no water supply to unit. Check galley water supply.

    Brew Handle is not in the ‘down’ position. Lower the Brew Handle.
