    FROTH MODULE
    REMOVE the Froth Module if the right side of the Espresso/Cappuccino Machine is to be used to brew. The right side brew functions will NOT operate if the Froth Module remains in the Espresso/Cappuccino Machine.
    BREW HANDLE AND POD HOLDERS
    RAISE the Brew Handle and REMOVE the Pod Holders from the Espresso/Cappuccino Machine. Discard any used espresso pods.
    POD HOLDER
    PLACE an ESE pod into either or both Pod Holder(s) and press down firmly on each pod. INSERT the Pod Holder(s) fully into the Espresso/Cappuccino Machine. PUSH until Pod Holder(s) “click” securely into place.
    RECEIVING CUP
    PLACE a receiving cup into the left space for a left brew and/or into the right space for a right brew. The receiving cups are NOT secured by the Brew Handle.
    BREW HANDLE
    PLACE the Brew Handle in the ‘down’ position. The Espresso/Cappuccino Machine will not operate if the Brew Handle is not down.
    OBSERVE the ON/OFF light ring is Green, indicating the unit is in the STANDBY ON mode, and indicators for all available brew options are steady on.
