    TRANSITION BETWEEN
    STANDBY ON & STANDBY OFF

    ONCE PRIMED, THE UNIT CAN BE TRANSITIONED BETWEEN STANDBY ON AND STANDBY OFF WITHOUT THE NEED TO RE-PRIME WHEN RETURNING TO STANDBY ON.

    WHEN THE UNIT IS MOVED FROM STANDBY OFF TO STANDBY ON, SOME TIME WILL BE NEEDED TO ALLOW THE HEATERS TO COME UP TO TEMPERATURE.

    WHEN THE UNIT IS IN STANDBY ON, IF NOT OPERATED FOR 30 MINUTES, IT WILL TRANSITION TO STANDBY OFF.

    OBSERVE the ON/OFF light ring is Blue, indicating the unit is in STANDBY OFF mode.
    PRESS the ON/OFF button once.
    OBSERVE the ON/OFF light ring is Green, indicating the unit is in STANDBY ON mode.
