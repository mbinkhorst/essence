    FROTH WAND CLEANING
    LIGHTLY CLEAN THE FROTH WAND PER THE RINSE INSTRUCTIONS IN SECTION 5D. OR, FOR A MORE THOROUGH CLEANING, FOLLOW THE INSTRUCTIONS BELOW.
    The Froth Wand assembly should be cleaned at the end of every flight, or after not being used for four hours or longer.
    OBSERVE the ON/OFF light ring is Green, indicating the unit is in the STANDBY ON mode, and all indicators are steady ON.
    FROTH WAND AND WAND PIVOT
    WIPE the Froth Wand and the Froth Wand pivot with a clean cloth.
    FROTH WAND ASSEMBLY
    1. REMOVE and DISASSEMBLE the Froth Wand assembly from the Espresso/Cappuccino Machine (6C).
    2. WIPE out the threaded opening for the Froth Wand base with a clean, soft cloth.
    FROTH WAND
    RINSE ALL Froth Wand components with clean fresh water.
    FROTH WAND
    SOAK ALL Froth Wand components in cleaning solution* for several minutes.
    FROTH WAND
    SCRUB ALL Froth Wand components with a cleaning brush to remove any remaining debris, as necessary.
    FROTH WAND
    RINSE ALL Froth Wand components thoroughly with clean fresh water.
    FROTH WAND ASSEMBLY
    1. RE-ATTACH the Froth Wand base to the Froth Wand pivot.
    2. RE-ASSEMBLE the Froth Wand (aerator and sheath).
    3. RE-ATTACH the Froth Wand to the Froth Wand base.
    FROTH WAND AND FROTHING PITCHER
    PLACE frothing pitcher of clean water (approximately 170-250 mL) under the Froth Wand, so the lower part of the Froth Wand is covered by water. This will prevent splashing water and excessive noise.
    PRESS and RELEASE the Froth button to run an automatic 60-second steam function.
    CAUTION: WATER WILL BE HOT!
    FROTHING PITCHER
    EMPTY the frothing pitcher by pouring it into the galley sink.
    NOTE: The platen drip tray is not intended for disposal of large volumes of liquid. Do not pour liquids into the drip tray.
    CAUTION!! KEEP ALL FOREIGN OBJECTS OUT OF THE PLATEN AREA AND DRAIN. BLOCKING THIS AREA WITH PAPER OR OTHER DEBRIS COULD CAUSE IT TO CLOG AND OVERFILL.
    * Cleaning solution may be any of the following:
    1. 500mL cold water with 5mL antibacterial dishwashing liquid.
    2. 500mL cold water with 15mL Full Circle® cleaning liquid.
    3. 500mL cold water with 30mL Rinza® cleaning liquid.
    4. Other milk cleaning solutions per cleaning product manufacturer instructions.
