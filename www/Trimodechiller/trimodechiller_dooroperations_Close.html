ESSENCE® Tri-Mode Chiller
    DOOR OPERATIONS
    HOW TO CLOSE THE DOOR
    LH - SAFE POSITION
    RH - SAFE POSITION
    Make sure that the primary door latch is in the
     
    SAFE or
     
    OPEN position and the secondary latch in the upper position.
    LH - CLOSE POSITION
    RH - CLOSE POSITION
    Push the door against the door frame. Turn the primary door latch to the vertical lock position to LOCK the door.
    LH - CLOSE/LOCK POSITION
    RH - CLOSE/LOCK POSITION
    Lower the secondary latch in the down position to close/lock the door.
