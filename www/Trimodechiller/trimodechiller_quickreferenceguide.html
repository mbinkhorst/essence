ESSENCE® Tri-Mode Chiller
    quick reference guide
    CONDENSATION
    To prevent condensation, the unit’s door needs to be in the LOCK position rather than in the SAFE position. The SAFE position should not be used.
    RESET UNIT
    Power can be reset by pressing and holding the ON/OFF button for at least 5 seconds rather than cycling galley panel circuit breakers.
    ON/OFF BUTTON SHOWS AMBER LIGHT RING
    For the case that the light ring of the ON/OFF button shows Amber color, reset the unit by pressing and holding the ON/OFF button for at least 6 seconds. If after resetting the amber light does not disappear send the unit back for inspection.
    DISPLAY ACTUAL COMPARTMENT TEMPERATURE
    Press and hold the MODE button for at least 3 seconds to display the actual temperature of the chiller compartment. Press and hold again for at least 3 seconds to cancel.
    SYSTEM STABILIZING
    The Tri-Mode Chiller cannot perform its cooling function due to an external condition (e.g. high ambient temperature).
    TEMP WARNING
    The inner cavity temperature has been above the mode temperature (e.g. too warm) for some time. Food quality may be degraded and should be checked by the flight crew.
    SERVICE REQUIRED
    The tri-mode chiller has experienced an unrecoverable fault and should be returned for repair/rework/service.
    OVERHEATING
    To prevent overheating, the unit’s external inlet vent cannot be blocked by paper towels or cabin crew notes.
    SYNC LOSS
    The tri-mode chiller displays this upon one of the following conditions: No software loaded, incorrect software loading switch position, bad cable assembly, or bad microprocessor PCB. Treat like SERVICE REQUIRED.
    RAPID PULL DOWN IN BEVERAGE CHILLER MODE
    In Beverage Chiller Mode there is a rapid pull down phase. This phase will reset whenever the mode is changed or when power is cycled.
    FAN
    When the unit is cooling and the door is opened, the fan will turn off. If the door is left open, the fan will automatically restart after 1 minute.
