ESSENCE® Tri-Mode Chiller
    DOOR OPERATIONS
    HOW TO OPEN THE DOOR
    LH - CLOSE/LOCK POSITION
    RH - CLOSE/LOCK POSITION
    Lift the secondary latch in the upper position.
    LH - SAFE POSITION
    RH - SAFE POSITION
    In the
     
    SAFE position, the door is mechanically locked, however the unit should never be operated in this position as it will reduce food cooling and will create condensation.
    LH - OPEN POSITION
    RH - OPEN POSITION
    Turn the primary door latch to the
     
    OPEN position and open the door to load or remove the content.
