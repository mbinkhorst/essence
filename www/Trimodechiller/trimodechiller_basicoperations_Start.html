    How to START a program
    The unit operates in three (3) modes:
    • Freezer 0°F (-18°C)
    • Refrigerator 39°F (4°C)
    • Beverage Chiller 46°F (8°C)
    To select the desired mode, press the MODE button repeatedly until the desired mode (Freezer, Refrigerator or Beverage Chiller) is displayed on HMI display.
    Unit mode can be changed any time.

    Note: Defrost mode is initiated automatically to remove ice formation in the refrigerator and ‘Defrost’ is displayed on the HMI.

    Note: The LED bar(s) ‘breathe’ (e.g. increase and decrease in intensity until the target temperature is achieved). When achieved the LED bar(s) are on continuously.
