    (IF NECESSARY)
    OBSERVE a Blue light ring indicates the unit is receiving power from the aircraft and is in the STANDBY OFF mode.
    OBSERVE when the unit is not receiving power from the aircraft OR when in A/C Maintenance Mode the light ring of the ON/OFF button is off. Check the display for ‘A/C Maint. in Progress’ OR check galley circuit breakers to energize the unit and wait for the Blue ring to light up.
    PRESS the ON/OFF button once, to switch from STANDBY OFF mode to ON mode
    OBSERVE
    1. All indicators each light up during the initialization process.
    2. After a succesful initialization, the unit will operate in the same mode as it was saved before shut down.
